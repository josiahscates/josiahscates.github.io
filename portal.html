Account & Security Rules

  • Every employee gets an individual login — accounts are never shared.
  • Two-factor authentication is required on all accounts.
  • Access is role-based — field staff, office staff, and admin see different folders.
  • Accounts are disabled immediately when someone leaves the company.
  • Company documents live only in Workspace — never in personal email, texts, or thumb drives.
