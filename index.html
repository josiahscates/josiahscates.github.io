Services

We focus on doing a few things well — putting the right house on the right lot, at a budget that holds.

Spec Home Construction

Market-ready new homes on carefully selected lots. Every build starts with comparable-sales analysis of the neighborhood — the right size, layout, and finish level for where it sits, not a one-size-fits-all plan.

About Us

Scates Global Solutions LLC is a family-run construction company based in Prairie Grove, Arkansas, serving the greater Northwest Arkansas region — Fayetteville, Springdale, Rogers, Bentonville, Bella Vista, Siloam Springs, Farmington, and the surrounding communities.

What sets us apart is homework. Before we buy a lot or swing a hammer, we analyze the market block by block — land costs, comparable sales, construction budgets, and absorption — so every project stands on numbers, not hope. The result: homes priced right for their street, built with the quality buyers can feel.

We believe real luxury isn't square footage or flashy finishes — it's proportion, light, storage, quiet, and a dry, tight, comfortable house. That philosophy guides every plan we draw and every trade we hire.
