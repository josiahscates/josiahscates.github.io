Who We Are

Your neighbors, who happen to build beautiful homes.

Olive & Atlas Construction is a family-run building company rooted in Prairie Grove and serving the communities of Northwest Arkansas — Fayetteville, Springdale, Rogers, Bentonville, Bella Vista, Siloam Springs, Farmington, and the towns in between. These aren't just markets to us. They're where we live, where our kids grow up, and where we run into our clients at the grocery store.

We started this company on a simple conviction: real luxury isn't a bigger price tag. It's proportion and light. It's storage where you need it and quiet where you want it. It's a house that stays dry, comfortable, and solid for decades. Those things come from careful planning and disciplined building — not from spending more.

So that's what we do. We plan every project like it's our own home, we tell you the truth about where money matters and where it doesn't, and we build the details you'll touch every day like someone's going to notice. Because you will.

The Olive & Atlas Promise

What we owe you on every project.

  • The truth about cost. A real budget before we start, and a straight answer the moment anything threatens it.
  • Respect for your home and street. Tidy sites, considerate scheduling, and crews we'd welcome into our own houses.
  • Craftsmanship you can inspect. Walk any stage of the work with us — we hold quality checkpoints before anything gets covered.
  • Guidance, not upselling. If a cheaper path serves you better, that's the one we'll recommend.
  • A phone that gets answered. During your project and after it. Our name is on this work.
